Background Story on how Ecohub was founded

2year ago I tried to venture into agriculture, to be specific poultry farming, and I used my savings and money from Helb loan to start the project and it failed twice which was so demoralizing . The project failed mainly because I lacked the required skills to propel the poultry farm alive and also getting hold of expertise advice was not possible because I did not know any and the ones I knew were hard to get to. This is where I came up with this idea, of creating an online platform where farmers like I am can get help on problems they have on agriculture before its late .
